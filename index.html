Can I make a reservation?


Due to popular demand, we only do walk-ins. While we do our best to accommodate everyone, please note that we can get quite busy, especially during peak hours.

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?


Yes, we have a variety of vegan and gluten-free desserts available to cater to different dietary preferences. Our menu is designed to ensure everyone can indulge in our sweet offerings.

What are your opening hours?


We are open from 9 AM to 5PM Mondays to Thursdays , and until 6 PM on weekends and Fridays. Please note that hot food orders end at 4 PM.
