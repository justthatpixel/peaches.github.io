Our Community

At Peach, we believe that great food brings people together. Our community is at the heart of everything we do, and we are proud to be a gathering place for friends, families, and dessert lovers alike. Our cozy and inviting atmosphere is designed to foster connection. Whether you’re catching up with friends over a slice of our signature cake or enjoying a leisurely brunch with family.

Sweet Flavors for Every Craving!

Everyone deserves a sweet treat, and we strive to ensure that our menu reflects the tastes and needs of all our guests.

Company news

Can I make a reservation?

Yes, we encourage reservations, especially for larger groups or special occasions. You can book a table through our website or by calling us directly.

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegan and gluten-free desserts available to cater to different dietary preferences. Our menu is designed to ensure everyone can indulge in our sweet offerings.

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 10:00 AM to midnight on weekdays, and until 12:30 AM on weekends. Please note that dining ends 30 minutes before closing.
